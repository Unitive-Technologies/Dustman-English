Who We Are

DUSTMAN WASTE MANAGEMENT & RECYCLING PVT LTD was founded with 7 years of industry experience. We have gained enough experience over the years to establish ourselves as an effective E-waste management company.We have all the certifications needed for recycling and scrap vendor activities.

We are a team of expert professionals, who have vast experience in the field of taking all kinds of scraps. Because of our extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of the industry, we are able to deliver all types of scrap to clients within the timeframe specified. In addition, we take a vigilant approach at the time of the transit of the scraps and offer these products with the guarantee of quality clearing and excellence in services. And we are financially fit for large quantities of scraps.

Dustman has all of the latest equipment to ensure a quick response to the client's needs and flawless execution. We only use high-quality, environmentally friendly cleaning products.

Our Goal

Our objective is to minimize and eradicate waste and hazardous components from the environment by enabling our electronic waste asset management. It is to formulate an effective waste management system by eradicating a massive problem in our society. We are transitioning our ecosystem to enable a regenerative and circular green economy while keeping the Sustainable Development Goals in line.

It's time to "re-use, reduce and recycle," as rightly said about the 3 R's. We envisioned enormous potential for recycling unwanted waste by revolutionizing the adaptation of technology to become a zero waste planet and trailblazing to create a green ecosystem. Our waste management is incorporated to customize treatments to reduce the accumulation of discarded electronics and lead toward a sustainable solution for our planet. It is a proactive initiative to reduce hazardous equipment that only contributes to landfills and waste streams in abundance. Our process is a true reverse logistics process, entailing the processing of waste from the end consumer to formal e-waste recyclers, therefore making sustainable and economic progress towards a safe green economy for the planet. We are creating a space of opportunity for people to transfer their existing electronic waste into socially and industrially beneficial raw materials like many valuable metals, plastics and glass by using simple, cost-efficient, and environmentally friendly technologies for further reuse and operations.
